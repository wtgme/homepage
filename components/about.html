About Me

I am Tao Wang, a Research Fellow in Health Text Analytics and Data Science at King's College London. My expertise lies in applying advanced AI and Data Science techniques to healthcare challenges, particularly in mental health.

My research focuses on leveraging natural language processing, machine learning, and knowledge representation to improve mental health services, advance clinical decision support, and address health inequalities. I collaborate with clinicians, NHS Trusts, and international research institutions to translate AI technologies into practical healthcare applications.

My academic journey spans multiple disciplines and institutions. I completed a Ph.D. in Economics—funded by the ESRC—at the University of Southampton in collaboration with The Alan Turing Institute, where I focused on online social networks and eating disorders. I also hold an M.Sc. in Software Engineering from South China University of Technology (SCUT), where I participated in an exchange program at the Chinese University of Hong Kong.

Research Interests

My research focuses on:

  • Natural Language Processing for clinical text
  • Health Data Science and population health management
  • Multimodal Learning in healthcare applications
  • Knowledge Graph construction and reasoning for clinical information
  • AI-driven Clinical Decision Support systems
  • Health inequalities and bias in AI systems
  • Mental health informatics

Professional Highlights

Over the years, I have contributed to numerous impactful projects and initiatives:

  • Investigating multimorbidity trajectories among people with severe mental illness
  • Developing systematic approaches to examine and address health inequalities
  • Leading research on medical timeline extraction using large language models
  • Developing AI-driven tools for identifying treatment-resistant schizophrenia
  • Developing VIEWER, a population health management platform for mental healthcare
  • Creating early warning systems for psychosis risk using natural language processing
  • Implementing CogStack and AI solutions in clinical settings across NHS Trusts
  • Advising Meta/Facebook on eating disorders and body image issues in online communities
