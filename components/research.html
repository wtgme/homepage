Research Interests

  • Clinical Natural Language Processing: Extracting meaningful insights from unstructured clinical text, including medical timeline construction and the modeling of clinical narratives.
  • Health Data Science: Applying advanced analytics and machine learning to large-scale healthcare datasets—such as electronic health records and social media—to generate actionable insights and inform personalized interventions.
  • Multimodal Learning: Integrating heterogeneous data types (e.g., text, structured data, and medical images) to build comprehensive patient profiles and enable holistic healthcare applications.
  • Knowledge Graphs and Reasoning: Building and utilizing medical knowledge graphs to support automated reasoning, enhance clinical inference, and improve decision-making processes.
  • Causal Inference in Health Research: Leveraging AI and statistical methods to investigate the genetic, biological, clinical, social, and environmental factors that contribute to complex mental health conditions.
  • Clinical Decision Support Systems: Developing intelligent, data-driven tools to support clinical decision-making and promote efficient, high-quality, and equitable healthcare delivery.

Impacts

My research has contributed to several impactful innovations in healthcare:

  • Implemented AI-driven decision support systems to enhance care delivery within the NHS
  • Designed tools to identify, monitor, and address health disparities in mental health services
  • Developed methods to support early detection and intervention in severe mental illness
  • Advanced natural language processing techniques to extract meaningful insights from clinical text
  • Created a population health management platform to enable proactive and data-informed care
  • Provided policy advice to Meta on body image issues across its platforms
