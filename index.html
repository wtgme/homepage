Welcome to My Homepage

I am a Research Fellow at the Department of Biostatistics & Health Informatics, King's College London. My research focuses on exploring the intersection of clinical research and AI to advance mental health outcomes.

My primary research interests include Natural Language Processing, Health Data Science, Multimodal Learning, Knowledge Graph, and Clinical Decision Support systems.

I currently lead several research projects funded by various institutions, including work on medical timeline extraction from clinical notes using large language models, and improving identification of treatment resistant schizophrenia using generative AI.
